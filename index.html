Arknights

A free-to-play tactical RPG/tower defense mobile game

Arknights is a mobile strategy RPG game set in a dystopian world plagued by a mysterious event known as "Originium," a dangerous substance that grants superpowers but also causes catastrophic infections and mutations. The world is divided into several nations, each with its unique policies and ideologies.
